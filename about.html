About

Portland based hair designer

Works at Chealsea Salon. Located at 2121 SE Belemont, Suite #A, PDX.

Pulling inspiration from her love of design, sculpture, music and all things unique, exotic, and luxurious, Ash believes that hair is powerful and deeply appeals to the artist within her. Continuously building upon her 9+ years in the industry and a myriad of advanced training with Vidal Sassoon, Bumble & Bumble, Aveda, Davines, Kevin Murphy, Schwartzkopf, Ash has mastered every texture and density of hair. She specializes in not only cuts, creative coloring and advanced chemical services, but braiding and extensions, as well. Her adventurous spirit and advanced knowledge of her craft make having an experience with Ash one to truly remember.
